About EOS Laboratories

Precision. Quality. Trust.

A manufacturer of microbiology, not a distributor.

EOS Laboratories manufactures microbiological culture media, diagnostic products and laboratory consumables for clinical, pharmaceutical, food, industrial and research laboratories. Based in Mumbai, India, EOS supplies laboratories across more than 40 countries.

Every product is engineered, sourced and released under our own quality system — not repackaged from a third party. That distinction shapes every decision, from raw-material approval to final lot release, and it is why laboratories trust EOS for results that hold up to audit.

Manufacturing conforms to ISO 9001 and ISO 13485 quality management systems, with performance qualified against reference strains defined by EUCAST, CLSI and international pharmacopoeial standards.
