Overview

One ring, a complete antibiotic panel.

EOS Antimicrobial Polydiscs carry several antibiotic discs on a single circular carrier, positioned at fixed radial spacing for clean, non-overlapping zones of inhibition. A single application places an entire screening panel onto a Mueller-Hinton plate, standardising disc placement and cutting preparation time.

Each antibiotic is manufactured to defined potency and released against reference strains, with disc-content and zone-diameter performance aligned to CLSI and EUCAST interpretive criteria. Panels are configured by organism group and clinical setting, and fully custom antibiotic combinations are available on request.
